What is NITMUN?

The National Institute of Technology’s Model United Nations is one of the most renowned MUNs amongst the NITs, and is a realistic simulation of what occurs within the United Nations. The purpose of NITMUN is to use diverse

attitudes from far reaches of the country to find solutions to real world conflicts,as well as to educate the participants in the areas of diplomacy and international relations, and to enable them to develop their public speaking skills.

Message from the Secretary General

Dear future delegates and well-wishers,
Welcome to the NIT Model United Nations 2016.

“Look at the hate we’re breeding
Look at the fear we’re feeding
Look at the lives we’re leading
The way we’ve always done before”

Not always that I quote ‘Guns N Roses’, but the world that we are living in right now can be best described by these lines. The territorial turmoil, rising intolerance and the never ending massacre of soldiers and children alike, things haven’t really changed from the past even though we now claim to live in a reformed and a digitalised era. At NITMUN 2016, that’s what we are all about this year, CHANGE!

The NITMUN 2016 is the evolved platform in the region for like-minded youths to discuss and tackle problems affecting the globe and raise their voice regarding the same. This year we’ve focussed on implementing big changes, because that’s what the world needs right now! A change from the old methods of dealing with social, economic and safety matters.

We have 3 councils this year, the UN Security Council with the agenda being ‘Resolving Territorial Conflicts’, the UN Economic and Social Council with the agenda ‘Freedom of expression of Religion’ and ‘Realization of rights of human refugees and resolving destabilization’. Plus this year we proudly introduce a ‘Fictional Crisis Committee Council’ for the first time in the NITMUN, which will be based on the ‘Ministry of Magic’ from the world of Harry Potter, so all the potter-heads are in for a treat!

In the past 4 years the NITMUN has attracted delegates from all over India and this year we grow our spectrum to even more colleges in the country along with strong delegations from schools as well. With a reformed structure and an experienced panel of chairs and executive committee you can expect the NITMUN to deliver the change promised!

So if you’re passionate about international relations and humanitarianism and love having fruitful heated discussions and experience the multiplicity of views from people of various backgrounds then NITMUN 2016 is the perfect place for you.

The venue for the NITMUN 2016 will be in the campus premise of the National Institute of Technology, Warangal on the 11th and 12th of March, 2016. We would love your participation and patronage.

Hoping to you see you soon!


Yours Truly
Shailya Tiwari
Secretary General
